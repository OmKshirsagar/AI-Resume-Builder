# ATS & Recruiter Standards (2025-2026)

## Semantic AI Parsing
- **Beyond Keywords:** Modern ATS uses LLMs to understand the *meaning* and *context* of experience.
- **Recommendation:** Weave keywords into achievement bullets naturally rather than listing them in a block.

## Technical Formatting (The "AI-First" Layout)
- **Single-Column Only:** Multi-column layouts are often misread by AI parsers.
- **Standard Headers:** Use "Experience," "Education," "Skills," and "Projects."
- **Text-Based PDF:** Ensure the export is not an image; text must be selectable.
- **No Headers/Footers:** AI often skips content inside Word/PDF header/footer sections.

## Recruiter Perspective
- **The 10-Second Scan:** Recruiters look for "Company Tier," "Title," and "Quantified Impact."
- **The X-Y-Z Formula:** "Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y], by doing [Z]."
- **Human Voice:** Avoid generic ChatGPT-style robotic summaries. The AI enhancement tool should maintain a professional but human-sounding tone.

## Implementation Strategy
- **ATS Checker:** A dedicated feature to score the resume based on these specific rules.
- **Recruiter Feedback Mode:** A Gemini-powered analysis that simulates a 10-second human review.
